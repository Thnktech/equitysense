Legal Disclaimer

EquitySense is a personal analysis tool designed for educational purposes only. It uses mathematical formulas to organize publicly available data.

  • Not Financial Advice: This application does not provide investment recommendations.
  • Data Reliability: Market data is fetched from third-party sources (Alpha Vantage) and may be delayed or incorrect.
  • User Risk: All investment decisions are your own responsibility. We accept no liability for financial losses.

🔑 Getting Your API Key

To pull live market data, this app requires a connection to Alpha Vantage.

  1. Visit AlphaVantage.co (Opens in new tab).
  2. Enter your email to claim a Free API Key.
  3. Copy the key (e.g., A1B2C3D4E5).
  4. Paste it into the "API Connection" box in the bottom-left sidebar of this app and click Connect.

📚 How to Use EquitySense

1. Portfolio Dashboard

What it does: Gives you a clear, distraction-free view of your net worth and asset allocation.

Key Feature: Use the "Blind Mode" (Eye Icon) to hide prices. This stops you from obsessing over daily fluctuations and helps you focus on business quality.

2. Profile & Health

What it does: Diagnoses You before it diagnoses your stocks.

Benefit: By taking the "Investor DNA" test, the system learns your risk tolerance. It uses this to warn you if a position becomes too large ("Concentration Risk") or if a stock is too volatile for your personality.

3. The Firewall

What it does: Acts as a rational filter for buy/sell decisions.

Benefit: It prevents emotional trading. Instead of selling because a price dropped, the Firewall asks: "Is the Thesis Broken?". It only suggests an exit if the fundamental business metrics (Growth, Debt, Margins) have objectively deteriorated.

About

EquitySense

Rational Capital Management System

The Problem: Most investors fail not because they lack intelligence, but because they lack discipline. Market noise triggers emotional responses—fear and greed—that destroy compounding.

The Solution: EquitySense acts as a Commitment Device. It forces you to write down your thesis before buying and mathematically validates that thesis before selling.
