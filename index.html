User Manual

Legal Disclaimer

EquitySense is an educational tool for portfolio tracking and behavioral analysis. It does not provide financial advice.

  • Data: Market data is provided by third-party APIs and may be delayed.
  • Calculations: Scores are based on standard financial ratios, not predictive models.
  • Liability: You are solely responsible for your investment decisions.

1. Core Philosophy

This application acts as a rational buffer between market noise and your capital. It forces you to define why you own a stock before you buy it, and checks if those reasons still hold true before you sell it.

2. Diagnostic Metrics Explained

The "Firewall" scores stocks based on five key dimensions. We do not use price action as a primary signal.

Growth Health

Measures the trajectory of the business. We look for consistent expansion in Revenue (Top Line) and Earnings Per Share (Bottom Line). A broken growth thesis is often the first sell signal.

Quality & Moat

Assesses management efficiency and competitive advantage. We analyze Return on Equity (ROE) and Operating Margins. High quality scores indicate a business that compounds capital efficiently.

Safety & Balance Sheet

Evaluates the risk of bankruptcy or dilution. We scrutinize the Debt-to-Equity Ratio. A low score here indicates excessive leverage, which is dangerous in downturns.

Valuation

Ensures you aren't overpaying. We use the P/E Ratio relative to the market baseline. While high quality commands a premium, extreme valuation drags down your safety score.

3. The "Firewall" Logic

The system generates a decision (Buy, Hold, Trim, Exit) by cross-referencing three layers:

  1. Fundamental Health: Is the business broken? (Low Vector Score)
  2. Thesis Check: Did it fail the specific pillars you selected? (e.g., You bought for Growth, but growth stopped).
  3. Portfolio Risk: Is the position size larger than your Profile allows? (Overweight).
