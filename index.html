1. The "Firewall" Concept

The Firewall is your single source of truth. It replaces separate "Buy" and "Sell" lists. It filters every stock through:

  • Thesis Check: Is the business doing what you bought it for?
  • Allocation Check: Is the position too big for your risk profile?
  • Valuation Check: Is the price justified by growth?

2. Behavioral Tools

  • Blind Mode: Toggle the "Eye" icon in Portfolio to hide prices. This forces you to focus on the business quality, not the daily P/L.
  • Pre-Mortem Exit: When you delete a stock, the app asks you *why*. This simple pause reduces panic selling.
