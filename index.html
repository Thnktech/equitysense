🚀 Getting Started

  1. API Key: Get a free key from AlphaVantage.co. Enter it in the sidebar.
  2. Profile: Go to the "Profile & Health" tab first. Take the 10-question DNA test. This sets your risk tolerance and allocation limits.
  3. Add Stocks: Click "Add Position" in Portfolio. The system will auto-select thesis pillars based on your profile (e.g., if you are a Dividend Investor, it auto-selects 'Safety').
  4. Update Data: Click "Update All". The app will fetch prices and fundamentals sequentially to respect API limits.

🧠 Features Explained

1. Investor DNA (Profiling)

We classify you into one of 15 types (e.g., Compounder, Risk Minimizer). This determines:

  • Max Allocation: How much % of your portfolio should be in one stock?
  • Weighting: Does the scoring engine care more about Growth, Safety, or Dividends?

2. The Firewall

A diagnostic tool that scores stocks (0-100) based on:

  • Growth: Revenue & EPS trends.
  • Quality: ROE (>15% is good) and Margins.
  • Safety: Debt-to-Equity (<0.5 is safe).
  • Momentum: Price trend vs 200-day average.

It then compares the metrics to your selected Thesis Pillars. If you bought for "Growth" but growth is slowing, it flags "Thesis Broken".

3. Buy & Sell Logic

  • Buy Candidates: High Score (>80) AND Underweight position.
  • Sell Candidates: Low Score (<60) OR Overweight position (Risk).

4. Currency & Reporting

Toggle USD/EUR in the header. Click "Report" to download a PDF snapshot of your dashboard.
